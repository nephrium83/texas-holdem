refactor: consolidate layer-2 crypto onto holdem/p2p/elgamal.py

Two equivalent implementations of the deck-encoding + threshold-ElGamal
layer existed: elgamal.py (committed in 4909424) and mental_poker.py
(committed in b324c31). They share the same design -- identical card
ordering, the same SHA-512 hash-to-group adaptation, the same threshold
ElGamal -- so keeping both would duplicate security-critical code.

Keep elgamal.py (the earlier, marginally more complete file: it has
card_label_bytes() and make_initial_deck(), the seat-0 round-0
operation). Ported the one helper mental_poker.py had that elgamal.py
lacked -- joint_public_key(), which sums per-seat public shares into the
joint key -- into elgamal.py. Deleted mental_poker.py and
test_mental_poker.py.

Suite stays at 132 tests (the 14 mental_poker tests were duplicates of
the 14 elgamal tests, which remain).
